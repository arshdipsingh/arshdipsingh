Gru


Gru is unceremoniously fired from his job at the Anti- Villain League, which sends his self-esteem crashing. When he discovers that he has a long-lost twin brother named Dru, Gru takes his family to meet his brother in the rural country of Freedonia where Dru owns the country's largest pig farm. Upon arriving in Freedonia, Gru is hit with an immediate dose of sibling rivalry when he finds that Dru, with his gorgeous full head of flowing blond hair, is his complete opposite in every way. But when the outwardly greatest heist the world has ever seen, Gru assumes the "big brother" role and his descent back into villainy starts to restore his confidence. .
